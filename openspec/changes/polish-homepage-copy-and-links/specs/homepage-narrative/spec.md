## MODIFIED Requirements

### Requirement: Hero section conveys personal/intangible framing

The Hero section SHALL be written as a short, tangibles-only introduction (name, role, affiliations, credentials) rather than a philosophical or narrative account of the person. Hero copy SHALL NOT use em dashes; use periods, commas, or parentheses instead.

#### Scenario: Hero copy is brief and factual

- **WHEN** a user loads the homepage
- **THEN** the Hero tagline is a short factual statement of role and affiliations (not a multi-sentence narrative about motivations or philosophy), and contains no em dash character

#### Scenario: Hero precedes tangible resume content

- **WHEN** a user loads the homepage
- **THEN** the Hero section appears above Experience and reads as a concise factual summary, consistent in tone with the Experience/Education/Skills sections below it
